Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to ensuring that your personal data is handled responsibly. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. We may share data with third-party services solely to provide our services (e.g., payment processing through Stripe) or comply with legal obligations. No data is shared for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Legal Basis for Processing Data

We process your personal data based on your consent and legitimate interests in interacting with our website. By visiting our website, you agree to our collection and use of your data as described, but you may opt out at any time.

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected:
